THE MARKET PAIN

Solving the SMB Cash-Flow Choke Point

Traditional collections agencies are slow, expensive, and burn bridge relationships. Small businesses (Property Managers, Trade Contractors) have zero leverage for past-due accounts between $1k and $10k.

🏢

Property Management

Firms struggle to collect tenant back-rent, move-out damages, and unpaid HOA dues. Enterprise collections take months and yield 30% or less.

🛠️

Trade Contractors

Plumbers, HVAC operators, and roofers regularly get stiffed on final invoices. They lack the administrative setup or legal budget to follow up.

⚡

The Contingency Offer

We approach clients with an irresistible 35% contingency fee pitch (we only get paid when they do). Ingesting their historical debt creates immediate runway.

Niche Testing Strategy

This validation phase is structured as niche testing. After determining which of these sectors represents the best fit for our AI dialing systems and offers the most profitable and productive path, we will specialize and scale within that single niche (assuming acceptable test results).

THE DEFENSE

Dispute Resolution & Exit Safeguards

For a 6-month trial, the traditional 5 D's (Death, Divorce, Drugs, Disinterest, Disability) are bypassed. Instead, we use shotgun exit mechanics to handle deadlocks.

THE SHOTGUN CLAUSE

Unilateral Buyout Option

If there is an irreconcilable deadlock on the strategic direction, or if either partner wants to exit early, the Shotgun Clause is triggered:

  1. Partner A offers to buy out Partner B's 50% share at a specific valuation.
  2. Partner B must decide within 14 days to either:
    • Accept the offer and sell their 50% share, or
    • Buy out Partner A's 50% share at that exact same valuation.

This enforces fair valuation and ensures a clean, rapid separation with zero gridlock.
